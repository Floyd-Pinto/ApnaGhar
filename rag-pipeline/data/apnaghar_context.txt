# ApnaGhar Platform - Context for AI Assistant

## Platform Overview
ApnaGhar is a comprehensive real estate platform providing transparency, security, and real-time tracking for property buyers and builders in India. The platform uses blockchain technology and AI to revolutionize property transactions.

## Core Features

### For Property Buyers
1. **Verified Property Listings**
   - RERA verified projects and properties
   - Detailed property information (BHK, price, location, amenities)
   - High-quality images and videos
   - Floor plans and project layouts
   - Trust scores and verification badges

2. **Real-Time Construction Tracking**
   - Weekly photo and video updates from construction sites
   - Geotagged and timestamped media
   - QR code-based secure uploads
   - Milestone-based progress tracking
   - Push notifications for important updates
   - Direct communication with builders

3. **Blockchain Security**
   - Immutable booking contracts stored on Hyperledger Fabric
   - IPFS (Pinata) for decentralized document storage
   - Tamper-proof construction media verification
   - Transparent payment and ownership records
   - Secure digital signatures

4. **Smart Booking Process**
   - Browse and filter properties by location, price, type
   - Virtual site visits and 360° tours
   - Online booking with token payment
   - Flexible payment plans (EMI, construction-linked, bank loans)
   - Digital documentation and e-signing
   - Automated payment reminders

5. **Investment Dashboard**
   - Track all bookings and investments
   - View construction progress in real-time
   - Payment history and upcoming dues
   - Document repository
   - Communication history with builders
   - Property appreciation tracking

### For Builders/Developers
1. **Project Management**
   - Create and manage multiple projects
   - Add properties with detailed specifications
   - Upload media (images, videos, floor plans)
   - Set pricing and availability
   - RERA registration verification

2. **Construction Updates**
   - Mobile-first QR code scanning for secure uploads
   - Upload construction photos and videos on-site
   - Mark milestone completion
   - Automatic buyer notifications
   - Progress percentage tracking

3. **Buyer Communication**
   - Respond to queries and concerns
   - Send announcements and updates
   - Schedule site visits
   - Handle booking requests

4. **Analytics & Insights**
   - View count tracking
   - Interest and booking analytics
   - Revenue projections
   - Buyer demographics

## Common User Queries & Responses

### About Booking
- **Q**: How to book a property?
- **A**: Browse properties → Select property → Click "Book Now" → Pay token amount (10%) → Complete KYC → Sign agreement digitally → Track construction

### About Construction Tracking
- **Q**: How do I track construction progress?
- **A**: Access your Buyer Dashboard → View "My Investments" → Click on property → See real-time photos, videos, and milestone updates with timestamps

### About Payments
- **Q**: What payment options are available?
- **A**: We offer full payment (with discount), bank loans (pre-approved partners), construction-linked plans, and flexible EMIs. Token: 10%, then milestone-based payments.

### About Blockchain
- **Q**: How does blockchain make transactions secure?
- **A**: Every booking contract, payment, and construction update is stored on an immutable blockchain (Hyperledger Fabric + IPFS). This prevents tampering and ensures complete transparency.

### About Verification
- **Q**: How are properties verified?
- **A**: All projects are RERA verified. We verify developer credentials, check legal documents, and assign trust scores based on past performance and buyer reviews.
